BUGATTI DIVO

The Bugatti Divo is a masterpiece of automotive engineering, blending luxury, speed, and exclusivity into a stunning package. Powered by an 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16 engine, it delivers an astonishing 1,479 horsepower, propelling the car from 0 to 100 km/h in just 2.4 seconds. With only 40 units ever produced, the Divo represents the epitome of exclusivity, appealing to discerning collectors and enthusiasts.
